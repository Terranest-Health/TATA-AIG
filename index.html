TATA AIG Network Hospitals

TATA AIG offers cashless hospitalization across 12,000+ network hospitals throughout India, ensuring easy hospitalization, seamless claim settlement, and quality treatment.

How Cashless System Works

When you buy a TATA AIG health insurance policy, you receive access to a network of authorized medical facilities where you can receive cashless treatment. This means you or your family don't have to pay treatment bills at these hospitals, as they're directly settled by TATA AIG (subject to policy coverage and terms).

Cashless Claim Process

  1. Locate a TATA AIG network hospital in your area
  2. For planned treatments, inform TATA AIG 3 days before hospitalization
  3. For emergencies, inform within 24 hours
  4. Bring essential documents (policy, insurance card, ID proof)
  5. Fill necessary pre-procedural forms at the hospital
  6. TATA AIG will review and release an authorization letter
  7. The amount is settled directly with the hospital

Key Benefits

  • No need to arrange finances for medical treatment
  • Freedom to get quality treatment without financial worry
  • Easy discharge process with direct bill clearance
  • Now available at both network and non-network hospitals (with certain conditions)

To find a TATA AIG network hospital near you, please contact us or visit the official TATA AIG website.

Health Insurance Claims Process

Understanding the claims process is essential for a smooth experience when you need to use your health insurance. TATA AIG offers two main types of claim processes:

1. Cashless Claims Process

Available at TATA AIG's network hospitals, allowing you to receive treatment without paying out of pocket.

For Planned Hospitalization:

  1. Submit the cashless claim form to TATA AIG at least 5 days before treatment
  2. Wait for the confirmation letter from the insurer
  3. Submit your health card and confirmation letter on admission day
  4. TATA AIG will directly settle the bills with the hospital

For Emergency Hospitalization:

  1. Inform TATA AIG within 24 hours of hospitalization
  2. The hospital will fill out and submit the cashless claim request form
  3. TATA AIG will issue an authorization letter to the hospital
  4. Bills will be settled directly between TATA AIG and the hospital

2. Reimbursement Claims Process

Used when treatment is received at a non-network hospital or when cashless facility isn't available.

  1. Verify all bill details before signing
  2. Collect all necessary documents:
    • Completed claim form
    • Insurance card/policy copy
    • Medical certificate from doctor
    • Pathological reports
    • Hospital discharge card
    • Original bills and receipts
    • Original pharmacy bills
    • Investigation reports
    • FIR/MLC copy (for accident claims)
    • NEFT details for claim settlement
  3. Submit all documents to TATA AIG or their designated TPA
  4. Processing takes about 21 days from document submission

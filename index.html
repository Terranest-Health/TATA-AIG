How Cashless System Works

When you buy a TATA AIG health insurance policy, you receive access to a network of authorized medical facilities where you can receive cashless treatment.

Tata AIG offers 100% cashless treatment at any hospital of your choice anywhere within India.

With the GIC's 2024 ‘Cashless Everywhere’ announcement, the cashless facility is no longer restricted to our network hospitals. You can get admitted to any nearby network hospital and claim cashless treatment under your TATA AIG health insurance plan, where we settle 100% of your admissible claim amount directly with the hospital.

Cashless Claim Process

1

Locate a TATA AIG Network Hospital

Easily find an authorized hospital in your vicinity from our extensive list of 12,000+ network hospitals nationwide.

2

Inform TATA AIG

Notify us 3 days before for planned treatments, or within 24 hours for emergency situations through our 24/7 helpline.

3

Documentation & Authorization

Receive your treatment without worrying about payments as TATA AIG directly settles the approved bills with the hospital.
